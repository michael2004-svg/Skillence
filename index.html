About Skillence

We Build.
We Ship.
We Repeat.

Skillence is an independent product studio based in Kenya. We don't do fluff — we identify real problems and build software that solves them, whether that's helping a student access quality education, a local shop owner get discovered, or a creator stay on top of their content game.

Every product is independently designed, built, and maintained. We are builders first — and we believe the best software comes from people who are close to the problem.

Ship fast, iterate faster. We launch early and improve constantly based on real usage.

Built for Africa, ready for the world. Local context, global quality standards.

Independence is a feature. Every product is self-sustaining, not dependent on VC validation.
